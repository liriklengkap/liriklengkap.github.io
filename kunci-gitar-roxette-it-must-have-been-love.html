2. how-have-must-been-rain-it-over-but-is​


Jawaban:

It seems like you're asking about rainfall. Rainfall is a major component of the water cycle and is responsible for depositing most of the fresh water on the planet. The amount of rainfall can vary greatly from place to place and from time to time.

Climate change is causing heavier downpours. As air warms, it can "hold" more water vapor than air at cooler temperatures. This is because climate change is supercharging the water cycle's evaporation process and messing with rainfall patterns. For every 1°C (1.8°F) of warming, precipitable water — or the atmospheric water vapor content — can increase about seven percent on a global scale.

In recent times, there have been instances of extreme rainfall. For example, in July 2021, a slow-moving storm poured over 4 inches of rain over west Germany, in what was described as the continent’s deadliest flood since 1985. During the same month, Zhengzhou, China, saw “once-in-a-thousand-year floods” over the course of three days.

However, it's important to note that weather patterns can change from hour-to-hour, day-to-day, month-to-month or even year-to-year. A region's weather patterns, usually tracked for at least 30 years, are considered its climate.

4. The child ate up all the meal we had served. He .... Very hungry. A. Will have been B. Could have been C. Must have been D. Would have been E. Must have


He (C) must have been very hungry. Arti dari kalimat tersebut adalah dia pasti tadinya sangat lapar. Must have been adalah frasa yang digunakan sebagai dugaan akan kejadian di masa lampau. Simak penjelasan berikut.

Pembahasan

Must adalah salah satu bentuk modal yang artinya harus. Must dalam penggunaannya secara umum menunjukkan sebuah keharusan atau kewajiban.

Contoh:

You must wear uniform clothes to school.The students must submit their assignment today.

Must juga digunakan untuk menunjukkan dugaan atau prediksi di mana ada sejumlah bukti tertentu. Dalam bentuk sekarang, must diikuti verb base. Sedangkan dalam bentuk lampau, must dipasangkan dengan have menjadi 'must have' diikuti verb 3.

Contoh:

The bag is really nice. It must be expensive.Last night, you looked so tired. You must have worked really hard.

9. apa bedanya must been dengan must have?!​


Jawaban:

must been dan must have merupakan "kata" yang bisa dikatakan sama . Namun yang membedakan keduanya hanyalah waktu pengucapan nya!

Penjelasan:

Misalnya Must been diucapkan saat sang pembicara mencoba untuk memberi kepastian seperti "I must been the number one!" ( saya pasti menjadi yang nomor satu!" ).

sedangkan Must have diucapkan saat sang pembicara hendak mencoba untuk melakukan sesuatu seperti "I must have number one" ( saya harus mendapatkan nomor satu ).

pada dasarnya Must Been lebih mengarahkan ke kata "PASTI" sedangkan Must have mengarahkan ke kata "HARUS"

33. apa bedanya must been dengan must have?!!!!​


Jawaban :

(Must have)

Must digunakan untuk menyatakan keharusan yang berasal dari si pembicara, sedangkan have to menyatakan keharusan yang berdasarkan situasi.

Sedangkan "Must been" itu setau saya gaada, yang ada itu "Must have been", "Must have to"

Jadi "Must been" itu gaada, penulisan yang benernya dari "Must been" itu dibelakangnya pasti ada tambahan "Have", ya tapi kalau mau tau perbedaan "Must been ( Yang kata "Must been" ini sebenernya gaada klo ga pake "have")
Dan "Must have" ya sama aja.

Have bisa diartikan sebagai : pernah,punya, dan harus, dll.

Must bisa diartikan sebagai : Pernah

Intinya kalau mau nulis "Must been" itu pakai "Have" ya dibelakang kata "Been" nya. Gaada bedanya sih sebenernya mau pake "Must been" kek atau "Must have been" kek ya sama aja. Tapi, kalo "Must have been" ya lebih bener dan lebih lengkap aja ya.
