Jawaban:

(?) were we at the party last night?

(-) we were not at the party last night

PembahasanSimple Past Tense

Past Tense adalah Tenses untuk menyatakan kegiatan atau peristiwa yang terjadi di masa lalu atau masa lampau.

Keterangan waktu Past TenseLast Night Last week/month/yearyesterday two daystwo weeks/Months ago

Rumus Past Tense

1. Verbal Sentence

Kalimat bentuk verba karena di dalam kalimatnya ada kata kerja (verb)

(+) S + V2 + Complement

(-) S + did not + V1 + Complement

(?) did + S + V1 + Complement?

2. Nominal Sentence

Kalimat bentuk Nomina karena di dalam kalimatnha tidak ada kata kerja

(+) S + tobe (was/were) + Complement

(-) S + tobe (was/were) + not + Complement

(?) tobe (was/were) + S + Complement?

Setelah membaca pembahasan mengenai Past Tense, mari kita bahas soal.

Soal:

(+) We were at the party last night

Structure:Subject => WeTobe => wereComplement => at the partyadverb of time => last night

Keterangan:Kalimat soal bentuk Nominal Sentence karena tidak ada kata kerja (verb) di dalam kalimatnya Dalam kalimat soal menggunakan Tobe Past (were) Ada keterangan waktu Last Night (semalam) yang menunjukkan waktu lampau.

Kesimpulan

Berdasarkan keterangan di atas, maka disimpulkan kalimat tersebut adalah kalimat Past Tense bentuk Nominal Sentence.

Berikut Rumus yg digunakan:

Past Tense bentuk Nominal Sentence

(+) S + tobe (was/were) + Complement

We + were + at the party last night

(-) S + tobe (was/were) + not + Complement

We + were + not + the party last night

(?) tobe (was/were) + S + Complement?

were + we + at the party last night?

12. they said "we didn't go out last night"they said that........ the night before


Subject : English

Category : Reported Speech



They said "we didn't go out last night"


Answer : They said that THEY HADN'T GONE out the night before.


Dalam reported speech yang reporting verbnya menggunakan past tense (said, told, asked, etc), tense mundur.


Jadi, karena kalimat langsungnya menggunakan past simple (S + V₂), kalimat tidak langsung menggunakan past perfect (S + HAD + V₃).
