1/3 + 55%

= 1/3 + 11/20

= 20/60 + 33/60

= 53/60

[tex] \frac{1}{3} + 55\%[/tex]

[tex] = \frac {1}{3} + \frac{55}{100} [/tex]

[tex] = \frac{1}{3} + \frac{11}{20} [/tex]

[tex] = \frac{20}{60} + \frac{33}{60} [/tex]

[tex] = \frac{20 + 33}{60} [/tex]

[tex] = \frac{53}{60} [/tex]

Kesimpulan pecahan biasanya adalah : 53/60
