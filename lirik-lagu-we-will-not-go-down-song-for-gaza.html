Penjelasan:

The song "We Will Not Go Down" by Michael Jackson contains several figurative language devices. Here are some of them:

1. Metaphor:

- "blinding flash of white light" - compares the intensity of the light to a blinding condition.

- "bombs fell down like acid rain" - compares the bombs to acid rain, which is destructive to the environment.

2. Personification:

- "Just a voice rising up in the smoky haze" - gives a human quality to the voice.

3. Hyperbole:

- "You can burn up our mosques and our homes and our schools" - exaggerates the extent of destruction that can be caused.

4. Repetition:

- "We will not go down" - repeated throughout the song to emphasize the determination of the people in Gaza to resist.

Overall, the figurative language in the song helps to convey the emotions of the situation in Gaza and the resilience of the people amidst the devastation caused by the conflict.
