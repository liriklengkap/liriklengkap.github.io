4. apa perbedaan dari loves,loved,love,to love ?


Perbedaan dari loves, loved, love, dan to love terletak pada penggunaannya dalam kalimat. Berikut penjelasannya.

Penjelasan:

Loves digunakan pada kalimat simple present tense jika subjek kalimatnya merupakan orang ketiga tunggal, seperti he, she, dan it. Contoh kalimat:

She loves dancing. He loves to visit her grandmother.  

Loved merupakan past form (V2) dan past participle form (V3) dari love. Loved digunakan pada simple past tense dan perfect tense. Contoh kalimat:

Dian loved singing when she was a child. I have loved painting for 5 years.

Love merupakan bentuk kata kerja dasar/ basic form (V1). Love umumnya digunakan pada kalimat simple present tense yang subjeknya I, we, you, dan they. Contoh kalimat:

I love drawing. They love to learn English.

To love merupakan bentuk to + infinitive. Bentuk ini digunakan untuk menyatakan kegunaan/ tujuan sesuatu. Bentuk ini juga dapat digunakan sebagai subjek kalimat. Contoh kalimat:

She learns to love herself. To love an animal is not an easy task for him.

Jawaban:

The passive form of the sentence "Everyone is going to love her" is:

(C) She is going to be loved by everyone.

Option (A) "She is being gone to love by everyone" is not grammatically correct.

Option (B) "She has been going to be loved by everyone" is not in the present tense and does not fit the context of the sentence.

Option (D) "She was going to be loved by everyone" is in the past tense and does not fit the context of the sentence.

Option (E) "She is going to love by everyone" is not in the passive voice.

Therefore, the correct answer is (C) She is going to be loved by everyone
