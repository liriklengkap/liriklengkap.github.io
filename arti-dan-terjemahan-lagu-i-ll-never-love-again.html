This is list of figurative language and the meaning:

Wrap me up in all your = make me yoursI want you in my arms = I want to hug youI was lost within the darkness = The writer of the lyrics was felt like he was trapped in difficult situation that makes him think that he is lost and can’t escape from it.Georgia, pulled me in = Georgia was attracting the writer and the writer want to know about Georgia moreI would never fall unless it’s you I fall into = the writer just want to be in love with her and it express how the writer how much he love her.

Pembahasan:

Figurative language is a way of writing with a creative tone. Dalam bahasa indonesia Figurative language dikenal juga sebagai majas. Figurative language terdapat berbagai jenis antara lain adalah:
metaphor, simile, personification, hyperbole and symbolism.Lagu Until I Found You oleh Stephen Sanchez adalah lagu yang ditulis oleh Stephen untuk kekasihnya. Lagu tersebut ditulis sebagai ekspersi bertapa bersyukurnya ia bertemu dengan kekasihnya tersebut. Makna kiasan yang terdapat di dalam lirik mendeksripsikan keadaan penulis dan bagaimana ia mencinai kekasihnya.
