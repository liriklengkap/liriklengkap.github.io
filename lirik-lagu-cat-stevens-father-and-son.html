16. Change into passive voiceA. My Father writes a letter to her son.B. Father and Mother read a newspaper


MAPEL : BAHASA INGGRIS

MATERI : PASSIVE VOICE


SOAL DAN PEMBAHASAN

===========================

A. My Father writes a letter to her son.

B. Father and Mother read a newspaper


=> Dilihat dari kata kerja yang digunakan di kedua kalimat di atas sudah di ketahui bahwa kedua kalimat di atas merupakan simple present tense. Jadi untuk rumus passive nya adalah :

Subject + tobe (am/are/is) + V3 + By.. Object


INGAT!!!

Subject di kalimat aktif akan berubah jadi objek di kalimat pasif.

Berikut penggunaan tobe sesuai dg subject nya :

Am => I

Are => You/they/we/nama orang plural

Is => she/he/it/nama orang singular.


Jadi kalimat passif nya adalah :

1). A letter is written to her son by my father.

2). A newspapet is read by father and mother.

"and when your father turns to stone"

The sentence is part of a song called House of Gold by Twenty One Pilots.

Below is just some part of the lyrics:

She asked me, Son, when I grow old

Will you buy me a house of gold?

And when your father turns to stone
