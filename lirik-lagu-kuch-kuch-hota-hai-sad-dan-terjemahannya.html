5. Kuis collab antara Tapasya & Gauri_______________________Lim x mendekati 8(6x + 5 - 4)______________________________Kuch Kuch Ho Ta Haicocok GK yha tapasya digabungin sama gauri :V​


Lim x ↣ 8(6x+5-4)

= (8.6) + 5 - 4

= 48 + 5 - 4

= 53 - 4

= 49

Jawaban:

[tex]\sf\bf\lim\limits_{x \to 8}(6x + 5 - 4)=49[/tex]

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

[tex]\sf\bf\lim\limits_{x \to 8}(6x + 5 - 4)[/tex]

[tex]\sf\bf\lim\limits_{x \to 8}(6x + 1)[/tex]

[tex]\sf\bf=6(8) - 1[/tex]

[tex]\sf\bf=48 + 1[/tex]

[tex]\sf\bf=49[/tex]
